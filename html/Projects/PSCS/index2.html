More about Us

At Custom Scent Creations, we specialize in crafting personalized fragrances that reflect your unique personality and style. Our scents are designed with the utmost care, blending premium ingredients to create a scent that enhances your presence and expresses who you truly are.

With our custom approach, you’re not just buying a fragrance — you’re creating a signature scent that will stay with you for years to come.

Why We Chose to Create This

Our journey began with a simple idea: to help people create a fragrance that truly represents them. We realized that a signature scent is a powerful form of self-expression, and we wanted to offer a service that allows individuals to curate their own fragrances. After months of research and development, we crafted a process that is both fun and personal for our clients.

Detailed Description of Our Product

Custom Scent Creations offers a one-of-a-kind experience for those looking to personalize their fragrance. Our scents are crafted from high-quality materials such as essential oils, natural extracts, and eco-friendly ingredients. You can choose from a wide range of base notes, middle notes, and top notes to make your fragrance truly yours.

Purpose of the Product

Our product is designed to help individuals craft a fragrance that matches their personality and lifestyle. It’s a personalized experience that allows customers to embrace their uniqueness through scent.

Materials

We use a blend of natural oils, floral extracts, and eco-friendly materials to ensure a premium, lasting scent. Our ingredients are ethically sourced and cruelty-free.

Features/Options

  • Choose from a range of base, middle, and top notes
  • Different bottle sizes available (50ml, 100ml, 200ml)
  • Gift packaging available
  • Ability to personalize the bottle label

Why You Should Buy It

Not only is our custom scent a reflection of your style, but it also serves as a memorable gift or signature accessory. Every bottle is created with precision, ensuring that your scent is unique and long-lasting.

Price

Our custom scents start at $45 for a 50ml bottle, with prices increasing based on complexity and bottle size. Discounts are available for larger orders.

Market Considerations

Supply

Our scents will initially be sold locally in Ohio, but we plan to expand to a national market within the next year. The product will be available both online and in select retail locations.

Target Consumer

Our target market is individuals aged 18-45 who are looking for a personalized, unique scent. This includes perfume enthusiasts, those seeking a signature fragrance, and individuals looking for memorable gifts.

Demand & Competition

While there is a demand for personalized fragrances, the market is competitive, with many big brands offering limited options for customization. We aim to stand out by offering a fully bespoke service with a focus on high-quality ingredients and customer experience.

Advertising Plan

We plan to advertise our product through social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest. Influencer partnerships and targeted online ads will help us reach our audience. We will also consider collaborating with lifestyle bloggers to review and promote our products.
